Mixed Waste

Electronic mixed waste refers to a collection of different types of electronic waste (e-waste). E-waste can include a wide range of electronic devices such as computers, laptops, tablets, smartphones, televisions, printers, and other electronic appliances. When these devices become obsolete or are no longer useful, they can be disposed of as e-waste.We are specialize in the proper handling and recycling of electronic mixed waste.
